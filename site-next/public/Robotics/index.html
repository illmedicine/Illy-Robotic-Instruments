Meet the Robot

Unitree G1 EDU — Standard U3

The G1 is a full-size bipedal humanoid built for research, education, and real-world demonstration. With 23+ degrees of freedom, high-torque actuators, LiDAR + depth perception, and an on-board AI compute stack, it walks, runs, balances on uneven terrain, manipulates objects, and interacts with people in real time. I own the U3 (Standard EDU) configuration personally and tune every behavior in-house before arriving on site.

Liability & Scope-of-Coverage Language (included in every booking agreement)

1. Insurance. Illy Robotic Instruments ("Provider") maintains Commercial General Liability insurance of not less than $1,000,000 per occurrence, covering third-party bodily injury and property damage arising out of the on-site operation of the Unitree G1 humanoid platform ("Equipment") during the scheduled demonstration.

2. Operator Control. The Equipment shall at all times remain under the direct supervision and operational control of Provider or an authorized Provider operator. Client, its employees, guests, or invitees shall not operate the Equipment except under Provider's express direction and supervision.

3. Safety Perimeter. Client agrees to provide a reasonable, obstruction-free operating area and to respect the safety perimeter designated by Provider prior to the demonstration. Client will reasonably ensure that attendees follow Provider's verbal safety instructions.

4. Exclusions. Coverage does not extend to: (a) damage caused by Client's willful interference with the Equipment; (b) losses arising from environmental conditions not disclosed to Provider in advance (e.g., live electrical hazards, unmarked drop-offs, hazardous chemicals); (c) cyber, data, or intellectual-property claims; or (d) consequential or indirect damages.

5. Indemnification. Each party shall indemnify and hold the other harmless for claims arising from its own negligence or willful misconduct. Provider's aggregate liability under this agreement shall not exceed the limits of its CGL policy.

6. Media & Likeness. Unless otherwise agreed in writing, Provider may photograph or record the demonstration for portfolio and marketing purposes, excluding any confidential client information, minors without parental consent, or restricted facility areas.

7. Cancellation. Cancellations made more than 14 days prior to the scheduled date receive a full refund less any custom-programming work already performed. Cancellations inside 14 days forfeit a 50% deposit. Weather- or safety-driven rescheduling is handled without penalty.

The foregoing is a summary. The full executed service agreement, including Certificate of Insurance, is delivered to Client upon booking confirmation and must be signed prior to the demonstration.
